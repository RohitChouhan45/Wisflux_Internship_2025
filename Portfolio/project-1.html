Project Overview

This is a personal finance tracker designed to provide a clear and concise overview of your financial health. At a glance, you can see your total balance, a breakdown of income versus expenses, and your average transaction amount. The 'This Month' section quickly shows your current balance for the month, while 'Largest Expense' highlights where your money is going.

The tracker also features visual aids to help you understand your spending habits. A donut chart illustrates spending by category, differentiating between expenses like shopping and entertainment. Additionally, a monthly overview chart tracks income and expenses over time, allowing you to easily identify trends and patterns in your financial activity. The interface includes a 'Dark Mode' toggle for comfortable viewing and displays the current date and time for easy reference.
