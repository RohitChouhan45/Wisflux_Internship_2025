Project Overview

A To-Do List is a simple, organized tool used to track tasks, goals, or items that need to be completed. It helps individuals stay organized and focused by providing a clear outline of what needs to be done. Typically, the list includes:

# Titles/Descriptions: Brief descriptions of the tasks to be completed.

# Due Dates: Deadlines for when each task should be finished.

# Priority Levels: Indication of how urgent or important each task is (such as high, medium, or low priority).

# Status: Tracking whether a task is "To Do," "In Progress," or "Completed."

# Checkboxes or Markers: To physically or digitally mark off completed tasks.

To-Do Lists are often used in personal life, work, or project management to improve productivity, prevent forgetting important tasks, and help achieve goals systematically. They can be created on paper, through apps, or in digital tools.
